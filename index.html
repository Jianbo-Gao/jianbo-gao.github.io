Jianbo Gao (高健博)        中文版

Associate Professor

School of Computer Science and Information Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University.
Email: gao AT bjtu.edu.cn

Jianbo Gao is an associate professor at Beijing Jiaotong University. Previously, he was a postdoctoral researcher at Blockchain Research Center, Peking University. He received his Ph.D. and B.S. in Computer Science from Peking University in 2021 and 2016, respectively, under the supervision of Prof. Zhong Chen. His research interests include blockchain scalability, smart contract security, and blockchain regulation. He has published more than 40 peer-reviewed papers at top conferences and journals, and served as program committee member in blockchain academic conferences. His research results have been applied in many blockchain platforms such as ChainMaker, Ethereum Classic, and Boya Regchain.
